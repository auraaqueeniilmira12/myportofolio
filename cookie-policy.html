Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 25, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how I use them on my portfolio website, and your choices regarding cookies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer, tablet, or mobile phone when you visit a website. They help website owners understand how visitors interact with their site and improve user experience.

2. Do I Use Cookies?

No, this portfolio website does NOT use tracking cookies or analytics cookies. I respect your privacy and believe in minimal data collection. This website functions fully without storing any cookies on your device for tracking purposes.

3. Strictly Necessary Cookies

Some basic cookies may be set by your browser or hosting platform for essential functionality such as:

  • Remembering your preferences (if any)
  • Ensuring website security and stability
  • Loading website content properly

These cookies do not collect any personal information and cannot be disabled without affecting website performance.

4. Third-Party Cookies

My portfolio may contain links to external platforms (GitHub, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, etc.). When you click these links, those websites may set their own cookies. I do not control third-party cookies. Please review the cookie policies of those websites separately.

5. Types of Cookies I Do NOT Use

Cookie Type Status Description
Analytics Cookies ❌ NOT USED No Google Analytics, no tracking scripts
Advertising Cookies ❌ NOT USED No ads, no retargeting
Social Media Cookies ❌ NOT USED Social links open in new tab without trackers
Functional Cookies ❌ NOT USED No login system, no user accounts

6. Managing Cookies

Since this website uses minimal to no cookies, you typically don't need to manage anything. However, you can still control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Site permissions → Cookies and site data

Please note that disabling all cookies may affect your browsing experience on other websites, but not on my portfolio.

7. Updates to This Cookie Policy

I may update this Cookie Policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology or regulations. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

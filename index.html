About Me

My full name is Aura Aqueeni Ilmira. I am 16 years old and live in Indonesia. Currently, I am a student at SMK Negeri 1 Ciomas majoring in Software Engineering. I have two main focuses in my daily life: website development and writing.

In web development, I am learning to build functional and easy-to-understand websites. I started from the basics like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and am currently deepening my knowledge of PHP and Laravel. Although still in the learning phase, I can already build some simple website pages. I believe that the learning process doesn't have to be fast, just consistent.

In writing, I actively write short stories and articles. I have sold story manuscripts on the Wizpen platform through a coin-based payment system. I also write for personal purposes, such as daily journals or story ideas that suddenly come to mind. For me, writing is a way to pour ideas and feelings into something that others can read.

Besides these two fields, I also have experience in school organizations. I was an active member of various student organizations, including student council and religious activities. From these experiences, I learned how to work in a team, communicate well, and contribute to the execution of various school events. I also helped collect and analyze Posyandu village data, such as data on pregnant women, toddlers, and newborns.

Outside of school, I worked as a counter staff selling credit, data packages, electricity tokens, and handling PPOB payments. I also sold digital products like e-books as a freelancer. These experiences taught me about responsibility, time management, and how to deal with customers.

I am a detail-oriented person who enjoys structured things. I enjoy the process of organizing, recapping, and analyzing information. In a team, I can coordinate quite well. I believe that communication and collaboration are key in every project, whether technical or non-technical.

My favorite thing is solving problems through code and expressing ideas through words. My daily habits are learning something new, writing a few paragraphs, and reviewing code. My motto is simple: "Be Nice! Keep learning, keep growing".

In the future, I want to become a better web developer and a better writer. Not the best, but better than I was yesterday. I want to build websites that are truly useful and write stories that truly mean something.
